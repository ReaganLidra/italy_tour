More Information

Blue Grotto is situated on the island of Capri. There are a lot of places to visit here! You may add this place to your bucket list along with your trip to the cave to enhance your traveling experience and journey. To visit the Blue Grotto and island of Capri, take an early morning ferry to Capri and book your group boat tour or private boat tour of Capri in advance to make the most of your time. Otherwise, reserve a private boat with a skipper that departs directly from one of the towns on the Amalfi Coast, Ischia, or Naples.
